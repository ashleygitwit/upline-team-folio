## Venture hypothesis

If we help independent P&C agencies turn renewal season into a repeatable, agent-approved outreach-and-recommendation workflow by triaging which accounts need action, doing the shopping legwork, and drafting a clear client recommendation, then agencies will retain more at-risk households and convert more renewals into cross-sell or up-sell conversations across their existing book of business, because today that work is too manual and time-consuming for most teams to run account-by-account at scale.

## Venture thesis

**Problem:** Every renewal deserves attention most agencies can't consistently give.

**World after:** Every renewal reminds customers why their agent matters.

**Our approach:** AI prepares the renewal. The agent owns the client relationship. Upline treats renewal as the agency's most reliable trust-building moment, not just an administrative deadline.

## Mantra

> Make every renewal prove the agent is in my corner.

## Upcoming proof point

For real renewals in this pilot, we need proof that we can repeatedly generate a proposal moment that agents trust enough to send and that customers respond to.

**Success:** Agent Trust · Customer Engagement · Scalability Confidence

### Recommended outreach email copy

**Version:** v8 (Session 4, Jul 6 2026)

One boilerplate for every renewal. Only variables: first name, renewal date, new premium (dollars only — no %, no old price, no increase language), and questionnaire link. Premium is never in the first sentence.

**Subject:** A quick review of your upcoming renewal

**Body:**

Hi [First Name],

At Members 1st Insurance, we value you as a member, not just a policyholder. As your renewal on [renewal date] approaches, we'd like to take a few minutes to review your coverage and make sure everything still fits your needs. Sometimes we find our members are missing out on discounts, because over time the information we have no longer reflects your current situation. Your renewal premium for this term is $[new premium].

One of the advantages of working with Members 1st Insurance is that we partner with seven different insurance companies. That means we're able to compare options across multiple carriers to help ensure you're receiving competitive rates and the coverage that's right for you. There's no need to shop your insurance with other carriers when we can do that for you right here. We work on behalf of our members to find the best rates — let us take the guesswork and the multiple phone calls off your plate.

To make sure you're not missing any available discounts or carrying coverage that may no longer match your situation, I've put together a brief questionnaire. Your responses will help us identify potential savings opportunities, verify your current information, and make sure you're properly protected.

[Complete the questionnaire here →]([QUESTIONNAIRE_LINK])

Thank you for being a valued member of Members 1st Insurance. We're committed to helping you protect what matters most while making sure your insurance continues to work for you.

[agent]

### Recommendation email (post-shop)

**Version:** v6.1

Post-shop handoff: rate comparison in paragraph one, simple comparison table, coverage reassurance, CTA to schedule a call (switch) or confirm stay (no shop). Agent reviews every quote before client contact.

**Status:** Active design target. Daniel P is the gold-standard switch example; first rec email draft pending agency review.

**Gold-standard example client:** Daniel P

### Key findings so far

- Outreach collapsed to a single boilerplate (v8) — agency wants one email for everyone, not per-bucket variants. Questionnaire carries nuance; recommendation email is the next frontier.
- New premium only — never show % increase, old price, or cause diagnosis. Clients flee sticker shock; broker-identity opener (valued member + seven carriers) is required.
- Shop threshold: prioritize ≥15% increases, ranked biggest-first. Email everyone on renewal as a relationship touch; concentrate shopping on big jumps.
- Small-batch sends (5–6/week) paced to quoting capacity — not all 15 at once.
- Quoting workflow: Upline quotes → Members 1st reviews before any client email → service team closes. Donegal→Travelers rewrite contract governs first move.
- Phone follow-up stays in-house — agency wants the personal touch on non-responders.
- First live quoting walkthrough (Session 5, Jul 7): Daniel P test case across Travelers, Safeco, Nationwide, Progressive. Home + auto quoted together.
- Sends ~1 week behind session rhythm — Week 4 catch-up Jul 20–24. Week 3 sessions start Jul 13.
