## Venture hypothesis

If we help independent P&C agencies turn renewal season into a repeatable, agent-approved outreach-and-recommendation workflow by triaging which accounts need action, doing the shopping legwork, and drafting a clear client recommendation, then agencies will retain more at-risk households and convert more renewals into cross-sell or up-sell conversations across their existing book of business, because today that work is too manual and time-consuming for most teams to run account-by-account at scale.

## Venture thesis

**Problem:** Every renewal deserves attention most agencies can't consistently give.

**World after:** Every renewal reminds customers why their agent matters.

**Our approach:** AI prepares the renewal. The agent owns the client relationship. Upline treats renewal as the agency's most reliable trust-building moment, not just an administrative deadline.

## Mantra

> Make every renewal prove the agent is in my corner.

## Upcoming proof point

For real renewals in this pilot, we need proof that we can repeatedly generate a proposal moment that agents trust enough to send and that customers respond to.

**Success:** Agent Trust · Customer Engagement · Scalability Confidence
