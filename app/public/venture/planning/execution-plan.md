## Venture thesis

Upline helps independent insurance agencies retain and grow household relationships by proactively reviewing coverage at renewal — surfacing savings, cross-sell opportunities, and carrier shopping when it actually pays off.

## Mantra

> Right recommendation, right time, right carrier — without the manual grind.

## Upcoming proof point

Week 1 pilot board: 15 households with tiered outreach (optimize-in-place under 10%, shop at 10%+) and live questionnaires — proving the renewal-review workflow end to end with Members 1st.
